## ?? ETHICAL PRINCIPLES

### 1. RESPECT FOR PERSONS
**Application:** All individuals mentioned in research are treated with dignity and respect
**Implementation:**
- No personal attacks or defamation
- Verification of all personal information
- Privacy protection for all subjects
- Right to be forgotten respected

### 2. BENEFICENCE
**Application:** Research aims to benefit society and prevent harm
**Implementation:**
- Public interest prioritized
- Harm minimization protocols
- Beneficial outcomes maximized
- Risk-benefit analysis conducted

### 3. JUSTICE
**Application:** Fair treatment of all subjects and equitable research practices
**Implementation:**
- No discrimination in analysis
- Equal treatment of all data sources
- Fair attribution of findings
- Balanced reporting standards

## ?? INFORMED CONSIDERATIONS

### PUBLIC INTEREST DISCLOSURE
**Rationale:** Research addresses matters of public concern
**Justification:**
